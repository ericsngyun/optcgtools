# Remote reference-capture kit — English Perona OP06-093 alternate art

For a remote contributor who OWNS the English Wings of the Captain OP06-093
Perona alternate-art parallel. GenkiStuff does not need to own the card; you
keep it. Your footage becomes Lane A reference evidence (never a
physical-measurement claim) and is stored privately, never published.

## What to shoot (one session, same spot, ~10 minutes)

Phone is fine. One fixed setup for everything:

1. **Lock the camera down** — prop the phone or use a tripod; do not handhold
   the camera. One lamp or window as the only light; don't move it.
2. **Lock the image** — tap-and-hold to lock focus/exposure (AE/AF-Lock), fixed
   white balance if available. HDR OFF, portrait/bokeh OFF, filters OFF,
   "scene enhance" OFF. Highest quality setting. Never edit the files.
3. **Background** — plain dark matte surface (dark cloth, mousepad). Card OUT
   of sleeve if safe; if it must stay sleeved, say so in your note.

### The shots

| # | Item | How |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Tilt video, 10–30 s, continuous** | Card flat under the camera. Slowly rock it about its horizontal axis (top-away → top-toward, ±30°), then its vertical axis (left-right, ±30°). Smooth and slow; all four corners visible the whole time. |
| 2 | **Front-facing still** | Card flat, square to the camera, no visible glare. |
| 3 | **Three macro stills** | As close as focus allows: (a) a ghost figure's foil, (b) the black nameplate area, (c) any border/corner region. Crisp focus beats closeness. |
| 4 | **Variant-verification shot** | Sharp still where the bottom-right card number `OP06-093 SR`, the ENGLISH rules text, and the vertical `Koushi Rokushiro` credit are all legible. |

Reshoot rule: if the card number text is blurry when you zoom in, reshoot.

## Rights declaration (required, one short text file or message)

> I own this card and took these photos/videos myself. I grant GenkiStuff
> permission to use them privately as visual reference for research. I
> understand they will not be published. — name/handle, date

State any limits you want. Without this declaration the media cannot be used.

## Sending

Private channel only (direct message/private link agreed with GenkiStuff —
never a public post). Send ORIGINAL files, not screenshots, not re-compressed
exports, not messaging-app "optimized" versions (use "original quality" /
"document" mode). GenkiStuff hash-records every file on receipt
(`optcg-reference add-media`), records your seller/uploader attribution and
declaration in the bundle manifest, and stores everything outside any public
repository.

## What happens with it

Your media is registered against the official card layout, scored honestly
(tier gates are strict), and used to fit a `reference-derived` appearance
profile. It is never labeled physically measured, never published as imagery,
and never resold.
